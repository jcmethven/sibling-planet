  • Great While It Lasted

    Let's face it - the Earth was a great place to live for anywhere between 6,000 years, if you're Catholic, or 200,000 years if you side with evolution. But the time has come for us to move off this rock to a sister planet in outer space. Global warming, an asteroid strike, nuclear war - it's only a matter of time.

  • Stop Wasting Your Money

    Walkathons, bowlathons, car washes, cookie sales, charity golf events, charity auctions - what good has any of it done? Every year a new hurricane destroys coastal villages. Every year another lunatic opens fire on innocent civilians. But we keep donating to charities, even though 75 percent* of funds are allocated for administrative expenses.
